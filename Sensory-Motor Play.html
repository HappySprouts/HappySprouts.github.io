What is Sensory-Motor Play?

Sensory-Motor Play Therapy is a therapeutic approach that uses structured play activities to help children process sensory input (touch, sound, sight, movement, etc.) while developing motor planning and coordination. Children with sensory integration challenges may feel overwhelmed by daily stimuli or struggle to interact with their environment. This therapy uses swings, textures, obstacle courses, and imaginative play to improve how the brain interprets and responds to sensory information.
