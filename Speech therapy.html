What is Speech Therapy?

Speech Therapy helps children develop communication skills, including speech, language, and social interaction. It addresses challenges like speech delays, articulation, stuttering, language comprehension, and expression. Therapists use songs, games, stories, visuals, and repetition-based activities tailored to each child's developmental level.

Key Benefits:

  • Improves pronunciation and speech clarity
  • Boosts vocabulary and sentence structure
  • Supports language comprehension and expression
  • Encourages social communication and confidence
  • Promotes school readiness and participation
