Playful Movement in Water

What is Hydrotherapy?

Hydrotherapy is a water-based treatment that supports children in improving mobility, coordination, strength, and balance in a safe and fun aquatic environment. The water’s buoyancy reduces pressure on joints and encourages fluid movement, making it ideal for young patients.

Benefits:

  • Improves muscle tone and strength
  • Encourages better posture and balance
  • Supports relaxation and sensory regulation
  • Fun and playful therapy atmosphere
